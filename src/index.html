ABOUT

Reflection

This is the reflection about my work. The best work in my portfolio is either the 25K website or the Elapse app design. The Team 25K website, while simple, is a close public representation of my current graphic design and web development skill level. It uses a wide variety of tools and techniques such as custom-made animations, smooth scrolling, responsive design, alignment, and colour theory. It also holds a special place in my heart as it is my own robotics team! The Elapse app design is another one of my recent best works as it is a large scale design covering many interfaces and interactions. While the app is still in development and the design is in the concept phase, I think that a good user interface is the basics for every good app, so it is very important here.


Some areas where I can improve on is writing higher quality HTML and CSS, and also improving my JavaScript skills. These are the three main building blocks of creating great custom websites, and there is always more to learn. I hope to improve these skills by learning online and also I hope to attend the Awwwards conference in May to get a more hands-on learning experience with experts in the industry.

Post Secondary

I am interested in pursuing a future in digital design or communication technology. There is a program of particular interest for me at the University of Toronto, Communication, Culture, Information & Technology (CCIT). This program will let me gain more knowledge behind media. For this program, grade 12 English is required as well as 6 other grade 12 courses. The average grade is mid to high 70s.


There are many pros and cons of this program. The program is related to what I want to do in the future, which is good. It also takes place at the University of Toronto, one of the best in Canada. However, there is a test after the first year which you must pass in order to continue in the program. The careers from the program include UI/UX designer, game developer, graphics designer, and digital marketing.

Reflection - cont.

I believe I demonstrated my learning skills this semester by being able to produce high quality work for each assignment, for example the Vogue magazine cover or even this portfolio. This shows initiative and independent work. I would try to improve my organization and responsibility skills!


Looking at the work I made this semester and in the past, I can clearly see the progression of skill, especially in making websites. My personally style is usually simplicity and a more monotone colour scheme, as I believe this way the viewer would be able to get the main points better; less is more. However, my specific styles are always changing based on inspirations from the places I go to and the things I see!
