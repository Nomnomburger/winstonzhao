ABOUT ME

Hey! I’m Winston. I’m a grade 11 student in Markham Canada, currently attending Unionville High School. As a student, I am passionate about learning and exploring new things, and I love meeting new people and exploring new places. I’m passionate about digital design and I’ve worked on several projects that showcase my skills in this field. User interface and user experience design is something I’m particularly interested in as I believe that the visuals and interactions greatly affects the quality and experience of a product. In the future, I hope to pursue a career in digital design and work for a company that values creativity and innovation. I’m excited to see where my passion for digital design will take me in the future.


Over the past few years, I’ve been learning and improving my skills at mainly UI design and brand design. The work I’ve done includes projects for clients, school clubs, and extracurricular activities. Through these projects, I’ve learned how to effectively communicate with clients and team members, how to manage my time effectively, and how to work collaboratively on projects. I’ve also learned how to take constructive criticism and use it to improve my work. I’ve also learned how to use various digital design tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma. These tools have allowed me to create high-quality designs that are both visually appealing and functional.

Robotics

As an extracurricular activity and hobby, I’ve participated in competitive robotics for five years. It’s given me valuable experiences in leadership, teamwork, and problem solving. I started competing in FLL on Team Polaris, where I was introduced to competitive robotics. Then I moved to VRC on team 839, where we qualified for the world championships twice. I then moved to team 3150A, where we won the 2022 Ontario Provincial Championships and attended the world championships as a completely student run team. Finally, I started my own team, 25K, to gain new leadership experiences. We won three awards and went to the world championships as a fully student run team as well.


Now, I’m focusing on media and communications with projects like The Drive Team Podcast and Magazine. You can check those out in my portfolio or learn more about my competitive robotics journey below!
